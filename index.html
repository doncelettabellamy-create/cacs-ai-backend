How It Works

A structured verification and reporting pipeline — from submission to audit-ready output.

1️⃣

Submit

Participant data and supporting documents are submitted through CACS or connected intake systems.

2️⃣

Verify

Identity, eligibility, and compliance checks are performed against program requirements.

3️⃣

Lock Evidence

All verification evidence is locked in the evidence locker — immutable and fully traceable.

4️⃣

Clear for Funding

A clearance status is assigned before any funding is released — giving administrators confidence and accountability.

5️⃣

Generate Reports

Audit-ready and outcome/impact reports are generated automatically from verified data — no manual compilation.
